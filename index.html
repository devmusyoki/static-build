About

We are a Kenyan law firm that brings together litigation and dispute resolution specialists to provide first-rate solutions to clients in Kenya and beyond.


We provide our clients with legal services to help them prevent legal disputes from arising and to navigate formal dispute resolution forums whenever legal disputes arise efficiently and assertively.


We mitigate risk while pursuing the best possible outcomes for our clients based on their legitimate and strategic objectives and targets.


Our capabilities include advisory and representation in disputes founded on commercial and tax law, corporate recovery, employment law, family and succession law, intellectual property law, land law, technology law, and telecommunications law amongst others.


With lawyers on the ground and networks beyond, our approach is steeped in local know-how while maintaining global and emerging standards of legal practice to handle the ever-evolving legal challenges of today’s world.
