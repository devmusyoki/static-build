Careers

Driven by our pursuit of excellence, we are always open to hiring talented individuals. We are committed to creating a productive work environment to enable our staff to achieve their personal and professional goals.

Diversity and inclusivity are important to us and we have taken active steps to embed these principles in our operations. We also strive to foster a healthy firm culture and encourage conscientious work/life balance.

ADRA Pupillage


What’s your ambition?

    Ours is to build a one-of-a-kind alliance of top tier African dispute lawyers. Want to join us?
    We are currently hiring pupils. To apply please email the following documents to hello@adra-advocates.com:


    • A cover letter
    • Your current CV highlighting any relevant work experience and extracurricular activities
    • Copies of your secondary and university academic certificates and transcripts
    • A copy of your national identity card or passport
    • Contact details of three Referees (from work or university only, must be or have been your direct supervisor
    • An essay of no more than 800 words on the topic of The Future of Legal Practice

    Deadline: 31st October 2022

    ADRA Internship


    Are you a third-year university student?

      Do you believe you have the qualities to excel in an environment where excellence is the minimum and loving what you do is mandatory?
      If you answered yes to both questions, apply to join our internship program today. To apply email the following documents to us on hello@adra-advocates.com:

      • A cover letter
      • Your current CV highlighting any relevant work experience and extracurricular activities
      • Copies of all secondary and university academic certificates and transcripts
      • A copy of your national identity card or passport
      • Three Referees (from work or university only, must be or have been your direct supervisor
      • An essay of your choice of no more than 800 words on the topic of The Future of Legal Practice

      Deadline: 31st January 2023.
