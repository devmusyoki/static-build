Introduction

ADRA Advocates LLP was established on 15th June 2022 by Anthony Leshan, Victor Mailu and Pearlyne Omamo.

Our firm sets a new standard of practice in Africa by only specializing in the field of Dispute Resolution, Dispute Avoidance, Forensic Investigations and Litigation.

Our firm’s key focus areas are commercial, employment, property, intellectual property, technology, telecommunications and family law litigation, corporate recovery and alternative dispute resolution.

Driven by data, technology and the ever-changing global environment, our firm offers its Clients solutions of the future, today.

We are delighted to submit our Profile for your consideration and shall be pleased to have the opportunity to work with you in the future.
